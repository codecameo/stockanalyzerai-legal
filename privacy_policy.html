3. Data We Collect

3.1 Account and Identity Data

When you sign in using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In (powered by Firebase Authentication), we receive:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • A unique user identifier (Firebase UID)

We do not receive or store your Google or Apple account password.

3.2 Usage and Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This includes:

  • Ticker symbols you search
  • Reports you view or generate
  • Paywall and subscription screen interactions
  • App open and session events
  • Device type, operating system version, and general region

Firebase Analytics data is collected in aggregate and pseudonymized form. We use this data to improve the App — not to build individual advertising profiles.

3.3 Report Content Data

AI-generated research reports you request are stored in Firebase Storage as markdown files. Reports are linked to your Firebase UID and accessible only via time-limited signed URLs. We do not sell or share your individual report content.

3.4 Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) assigns your device a registration token. We use this token solely to send you notifications about report completion. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

3.5 Subscription and Purchase Data

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives:

  • Your Firebase UID (to link purchases to your account)
  • Purchase receipts from Apple App Store or Google Play (validated server-side)
  • Subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)

RevenueCat does not receive your full payment card details. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google.

3.6 Crash and Error Data

We use Sentry for crash reporting and error tracking. Sentry may collect:

  • Device model, operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack traces and error messages at the time of a crash
  • Your Firebase UID (to correlate errors with user sessions, for support purposes)

Sentry data is used exclusively for debugging and improving App stability.

3.7 Local Device Storage

The App stores certain data locally on your device only — this data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Cached reports: AI-generated reports you have viewed are stored locally for offline access
  • Watchlist tickers: Stock ticker symbols you save to your personal watchlist are stored locally on your device
  • In-progress job identifiers: A temporary identifier is stored locally to allow the App to resume an in-progress report if the App is closed and reopened
  • App preferences: Basic app settings stored via standard Android/iOS preferences storage

This local data is removed when you uninstall the App or delete your account.

3.8 Data You Do Not Provide

We do not collect:

  • Your investment portfolio, brokerage account details, or trading history
  • Government ID, Social Security Number, or financial account numbers
  • Your precise GPS location
  • Payment card numbers (these go directly to Apple/Google)
